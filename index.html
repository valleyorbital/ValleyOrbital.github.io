About Valley Orbital

Valley Orbital LLC was founded to solve a gap that keeps showing up in the small satellite industry: engineering teams with deep hardware expertise that need IT infrastructure built and maintained — but don't have time to become sysadmins.

We're based in San Joaquin County, CA and work remotely with satellite operators across the country. Our background is in IT infrastructure, Linux systems, cloud architecture, and automation — applied directly to the ground segment.

If your team is tracking passes, handling telemetry, or managing remote stations, we can help.
